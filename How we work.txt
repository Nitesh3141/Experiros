1. Client visits the website, explore our work and generate a preliminary estimate.
2. If the client is interested, he/she can register with us using their email and mobile number both OTP verified. The client will be required to pay a refundable deposit of Rs. 1000 as a token of seriousness at the time of registration. This amount will either be adjusted in our fees later or refunded if we are not able to meaningfully engage with the client.
3. After registration, the client will be asked for more details of the house and will generate an estimate based on the size of the house.
4. The client will then book a meeting with us, where we understand client's requirements in detail. We call this coffee meeting.
5. the client enters into an agreement with us.
6. We start working on design options.
7. Client approves the design.
8. We start construction
9. We deliver a happy home to the client.
