1. HEADING: Explore & Preliminary Estimate

TEXT: Begin by exploring our portfolio to understand our design philosophy, execution standards, and completed projects.

Share your basic project details — floor plan, home size, location, and broad requirements — and receive a preliminary budget estimate to understand the expected investment range.

Purpose: Early-stage budget clarity with zero obligation.

2️⃣ HEADING: Register & Token Confirmation

TEXT: To proceed further, register using your OTP-verified email and mobile number.

A fully refundable ₹1,000 token deposit confirms your intent to move forward and enables us to initiate the detailed estimation process based on the inputs you provide.

Purpose: Structured onboarding with mutual seriousness

3️⃣ HEADING: Consultation Meeting

TEXT: Once registered, we schedule a detailed consultation (in-person or virtual) to understand your lifestyle, functional needs, aesthetic preferences, and budget expectations.

This discussion allows us to align your aspirations with practical execution considerations before moving into detailed planning.

Purpose: Understanding your vision before defining the scope.

4️⃣ HEADING: Requirement Finalization

TEXT: Post consultation, we consolidate all inputs and clearly define the project scope.

This includes areas to be designed, material preferences, storage requirements, customization elements, and execution expectations. Every detail is documented to ensure clarity and alignment.

Purpose: Clear scope definition before financial detailing.

5️⃣ HEADING: Detailed Estimate

TEXT: Based on the finalized requirements, we prepare a comprehensive and transparent detailed estimate tailored specifically to your project.

The estimate outlines scope inclusions, material specifications, execution components, and overall investment value — ensuring there are no ambiguities or hidden elements.

Purpose: Complete financial clarity before commitment.

6️⃣ HEADING: Agreement & Project Kick-Off

TEXT: Upon approval of the estimate, we formalize the engagement through a structured agreement outlining scope, timelines, payment milestones, and quality benchmarks.

This stage officially initiates the design and execution journey.

Purpose: Professional commitment with defined accountability.

7️⃣ HEADING: Design Development & Approval

TEXT: Our design team develops detailed layouts, curated material palettes, and 3D visualizations aligned with the approved scope and budget.

You review the concepts, share feedback, and approve the final design before execution begins.

Purpose: Design certainty before construction investment.

8️⃣ HEADING: Execution & Project Management

TEXT: Our skilled craftsmen execute the approved design under disciplined project management and quality supervision.

You receive regular progress updates while we ensure strict adherence to timelines and standards.

Purpose: Controlled execution with complete transparency.

9️⃣ HEADING: Final Handover

TEXT: After thorough inspections and finishing checks, we deliver your completed home aligned with the approved scope, budget, and committed timelines.

Your vision is transformed into a space built to last.

Purpose: Seamless transition from project to possession
